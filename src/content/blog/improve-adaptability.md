**Pain**

- Processes are hard to change
- Systems are brittle and subject to breakage when changed
- Decision making is only done by a select few
- Change is hard to manifest 
- Resistance to change is prolific
- Significant change (internal or external) can disable strategy or worse

**Profit**

- Processes and systems easily adapt to new scenarios
- Systems learn and become resilient and can repair themselves
- Decisions are democratized across employees, systems, and devices
- Change is much more fluid
- Large changes are much easier to plan, orchestrate, and implement
- Unplanned change is profiled and managed within Demand Management

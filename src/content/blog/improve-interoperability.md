**Pain**

- Integration with systems requires a lot of support
- Constant changes to systems disrupt handoffs to 3rd parties
- Designing integrations between systems requires long lead times
- Data issues constantly disrupt systems reliability
- Collaboration with people and devices remains a huge challenge

**Profit**

- Integration is efficient, effective, and reliable
- Systems and devices collaborate in a seamless manner
- Information and commands flow easily and predictably 
- Interoperability is a core competency for the organization

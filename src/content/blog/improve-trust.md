Pain

- Data integrity and analytics require frequent review & resolution
- Decisions are only made at the top level
- Executives are often not held accountable
- Culture of mistrust and excuses
- Low morale amongst employees 
- Customer and employee retention are a concern
- Product and service reviews are not in the top tier

Profit

- Data, metrics, and analytics are reliable and high quality
- Decision making is democratized across the organization
- Morale is consistently high while staff turnover is insignificant
- Accountability and empowerment has replaced policy and surveillance
- Customers are extremely loyal to our products & services 
- Growth is typically driven by customer-to-customer references 
- The organization is seen as a pillar of integrity in the communities we serve

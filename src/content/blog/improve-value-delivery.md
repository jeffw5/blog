**Pain**

- Product sales are lagging behind competitors
- New product launch did not meet expectations
- Our services always meet SLAs but we are losing market share
- We have not been able to expand our current share or enter new markets
- Our customer base has been shrinking for the last few years
- We hired a coach to boost sales, but we new product sales continue to slide

**Profit**

- When we launch new products, customers are already lined up
- We don’t spend a lot of money on advertising since our customers market our products and services for us
- When we enter new markets we don’t worry about the competition
- We are seen as the market leaders in almost every category
- We collaborate with upstream and downstream companies to help them improve the value of their products and services across the value chain
- Other verticals and industries often request our assistance and value our advisory services and insights

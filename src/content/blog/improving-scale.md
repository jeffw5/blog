**Pain**

- Hard to find good talent to push growth areas
- Too expensive to add new staff and/or facilities 
- Expanding existing markets for existing products is too hard
- Plants and equipment are specialized by product or location
- Training is inconsistent and quality suffers when we scale-up

**Profit**

- Talent is developed and groomed to drive growth
- Strategic sourcing and facilities support fast deployment and ramp-up
- Growth planning model eases market expansion & development
- Plants, product lines, and facilities are designed for adaptability and scale
- Investments in training and employee development provide big payoffs

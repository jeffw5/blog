**Pain**

- The number of systems and data islands is out of control
- Meetings consume almost 100% of executives and senior leader time
- Many middle managers and key staff are often caught up in meetings
- New functionality requires significant planning and investment 
- 80% of our project time is fixing systems or backfilling requirements

**Profit**

- Systems and data flow seamlessly into knowledge warehouses
- Systems learn to patterns of change and respond without intervention
- Large changes are easy to plan, prioritize, and rationalize into portfolios

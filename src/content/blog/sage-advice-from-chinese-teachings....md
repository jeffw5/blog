Just as we discover new meanings in the ancient writings, we can find relevancy in the teachings from thousands of years ago that seem to play out in every day business and life.  Here are a few that resonate on a variety of levels:

- By showing mercy, we develop great strength and courage
- Through frugality, we develop the capacity for generosity
- Great leadership is developed from those who demonstrate humility

You can study these and think about the deeper ramifications of embracing these three qualities to improve your own capabilities as a leader, as a parent/partner, and as a friend.
